The Best Moves According to Sam

Top 3 Action Movies of All-Time

1. John Wick

Keanu Reeves stars as a retired hitman seeking vengeance for the killing of his beloved dog, a final gift from his deceased wife. The film is renowned for its stylish action sequences and world-building.

2. Mad Max: Fury Road

In a post-apocalyptic wasteland, Max teams up with Furiosa to escape a tyrannical warlord. The movie is praised for its high-octane action, practical effects, and feminist themes.

3. Kingsman: The Secret Service

A spy organisation recruits a promising street kid into the agency's training program, while a global threat emerges from a twisted tech genius. The film is known for its blend of humor, style, and over-the-top action.
